BORN IN BRUSSELS

A Heritage of Epicurean Excellence

Our journey began in the vibrant, cobblestone streets near the Grand Place of Brussels. What started as an intimate gathering of culinary minds has blossomed into SvS—a sanctuary for fine dining. We are deeply rooted in Belgian tradition, sourcing the finest local ingredients from the lush Ardennes forests to the historic Trappist abbeys. Our mission is to honor our European heritage while pushing the boundaries of modern gastronomy.
